Inteligentné systémy v informatike (Intelligent information systems)

About us

SWe conduct fundamental and applied research in two major areas: system modelling and data analysis & interpretation. In area of system modelling our system development covers IoT, wireless communication and sensor networks. Research applications of data analysis include: expert systems, feature selection methods, bankruptcy prediction, handwriting analysis, interpretation of biomedical signal and data etc. We support interdisciplinarity and topics that bridges research from several departments or universities. Our lab was formed just recently in 2015 so we are still full of energy and we gladly welcome new team members or collaborators.
